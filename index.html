Bio

Ever since playing with clip art on the family computer in 1986, Joshua has been fascinated with layout and design. Soon he graduated from MS Paint to Photoshop and Illustrator, and fell in love with logo and symbol design on top of page and book layout. While learning to play bass in high school may have appeared to divert his passions, he soon realized bands require a lot of graphic design; t-shirts, stickers, album covers and more.

In fact, throughout his life he’s realized just how intrinsic design is to all arenas of his life. This lead him to pursue design more professionally working in fashion, making everything from t-shirts to hangtags and label tabs. More than anything else, however, it’s been gaming that’s received the brunt of his attention.

Around the same time as he was poking around on the family computer, his brother got him hooked into classic dice and paper role playing games. Fast forward almost thirty years where Joshua and his brother have been involved in the games industry for over fifteen of those years.

Working with the motto “I make evil things pretty”, Joshua now boasts a career of two decades designing for games, fashion and music, and he’s always interested to see where his passion for design will take him next.

PS: Yes, his middle name is actually "Brain". It's amazing what a typo on your birth certificate can accomplish
